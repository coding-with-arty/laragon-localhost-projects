Our Story

Who We Are

The Augusta Downtown Alliance is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering a vibrant and thriving downtown Augusta. As the heart of Maine's capital city, we work to enhance the economic and cultural vitality of our downtown district. 

2011

Articles of Incorporation filed: 8/9/2011 - "To promote the historic preservation, protection and use of Augusta's traditional downtown area, including that area's commercial, civic and religious enterprises and residences and to disseminate information of and promote interest in the preservation, history, culture, architecture and public use of Augusta's traditional downtown area, and adhering to its mission."

2013

Became an Accredited Main Street: 2013

2016

First annual River of Trees, November 25, 2016 - Now an annual holiday staple, River of Trees involves the community, a fun atmosphere, and serves as one of the largest fundraisers for the Augusta Downtown Alliance.

2018

First Trick or Treat on Water Street - October 31, 2018 - Previously called "Howls and Hounds" 2018 was the year this event became "Trick or Treat on Water Street". This is now one of the Augusta Downtown Alliance's flagship events!

2019

Water Street reopened for two-way traffic, increasing accessibility to downtown.

2021

T-Mobile Hometown Grant Sturgeon Project, 2021: Augusta Downtown Alliance, through the generosity of T-Mobile’s Hometown Grant, installed 26 fiberglass sturgeon on Water Street for 3 years. In 2024, the sturgeon statues were auctioned off, and the proceeds were split with the artists who painted and created them.

2023

Sturgeon featured on christening of USS Augusta - September 30, 2023

Our Mission

The Augusta Downtown Alliance fosters a vibrant, welcoming community by enhancing the economic, cultural, and social vitality of downtown Augusta. We achieve this through strategic partnerships, innovative programming, and preservation of our historic downtown character.

Our Vision

We envision a downtown Augusta that is the thriving heart of our region—a welcoming, walkable, and economically vibrant destination where people live, work, shop, dine, and gather to celebrate our unique community.

Core Values

Economic Excellence

Advancing Augusta’s growth through strong local business support and sustainable development.

Community Engagement

Building connections that strengthen relationships between residents, visitors, and local businesses.

Innovation

Driving creative solutions that enhance Augusta’s downtown experience and future potential.

Culturally Inclusive

Celebrating the diversity of our community by welcoming all voices and traditions into our shared downtown identity.
