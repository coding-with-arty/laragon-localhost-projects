Explore Augusta Outdoors

Discover the natural beauty and excitement that surround Augusta. From riverside trails and scenic parks to local festivals and outdoor adventures, there’s always something to experience beyond downtown. Whether you enjoy hiking, kayaking, or simply taking in the view, Augusta’s outdoor attractions invite you to get outside, explore, and be part of the vibrant life that defines our community.
